ABOUT US

We are a new format studio, so here we will not write about the things that are a must have for an art tattoo studio - sterility, disposable tools, biological material handling.
We do not limit you to choosing a sketch library on the site. If you know what you want, it is easy to find a sketch on the Internet, if there is only an idea - then together with our artist you can design a sketch so that you will be delighted. If you don't even have an idea and really want a tattoo - we always have sketches that our masters are ready to make for their own portfolio.

If you know what you want and know about tattoos - then welcome to us. If you are still hesitant or worth it, it is certainly worth it. Come and we will help you to make sure. If you have not thought of a tattoo at all - welcome to the tattoo world - let's get acquainted. We are sure that the tattoo on the original sketch will be read by you. Visit us in the studio or on social networks. If you have been wanting a dad for a long time, but have some cautions or fears - welcome to the studio where you can see with your own eyes and see that there is nothing to be afraid of.
